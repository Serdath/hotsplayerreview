Heroes of the Storm Players

This tier list is purely subjective and depends on my experience playing with that player. I won't talk about people with whom I have never played nor the one I have too few games to conduct a proper review.

My IGN is : DarkPrelateZ and I am currently playing in EU server. I identify myself as a hardcore player, always strive to become better at this game and take most of the matches I participate in seriously. It doesn't mean that I don't know how to have fun or troll but only when victory is assured. It is a game afterall and having fun is the utmost priority. But it is also a team game so makes sure your goal in a match aligns with other players. You may have fun by trolling but not every one in the team thinks the same.

My highest rank achieved is GrandMaster 18: 8148RP. My moto is : Everyone makes mistakes so every game is winnable, it depends on if you truly want to win or not or if your mentality is steady enough to think through during the struggle.

This is not really a tier list but rather my review list requested by one of my friend because they are curious about my perspective of everyone.
