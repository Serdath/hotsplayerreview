Heroes of the Storm Players

This tier list is purely subjective and depends on my experience playing with that player. I won't talk about people with whom I have never played nor the one I have too few games to conduct a proper review.

My IGN is : DarkPrelateZ and I am currently playing in EU server. I identify myself as a hardcore player, always strive to become better at this game and take most of the matches I participate in seriously. It doesn't mean that I don't know how to have fun or troll but only when victory is assured. It is a game afterall and having fun is the utmost priority. But it is also a team game so makes sure your goal in a match aligns with other players. You may have fun by trolling but not every one in the team thinks the same.

My highest rank achieved is GrandMaster 18: 8148RP. My moto is : Everyone makes mistakes so every game is winnable, it depends on if you truly want to win or not or if your mentality is steady enough to think through during the struggle.

This is not really a tier list but rather my review list requested by one of my friend because they are curious about my perspective of everyone.

I enjoy playing with

FrozenZerg

He is by far the most conscious Quick Match player I have ever met. Despite playing only Tychus and sometimes Butcher, He is willing to do whatever necessary to facilitate team's advantages by soaking or taking camps if his teammates happen to drown in ARAM too much. He also displays exceptional skills and adaptabilities with his favorite hero Tychus so you can always be assured he is fulfilling his role competently. From my experience, he is a hardcore player, focuses on winning rather than messing around and trolling, just like my style. I enjoy playing with him.

SamDovl

His Chromie is quite impressive, and he is an outgoing person, evident by constantly spamming emote at the start and at the end of the game. He also asks me (or you) nicely to join his lobby sometimes, which is appreciated instead of immediately inviting the player. He loves hunting Abathur and will gladly dive a fort to do so. Although sometimes by doing those risky moves, he forgets to soak, clear waves, and maybe even die. Still, he makes up for his shortcomings by timing his Time Traps perfectly to save his teammates multiple times. I enjoy playing in the same lobby with him.
