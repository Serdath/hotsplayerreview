Heroes of the Storm Players

This tier list is purely subjective and depends on my experience playing with that player. I won't talk about people with whom I have never played nor the one I have too few games to conduct a proper review.

My IGN is : DarkPrelateZ/Serdath and I am currently playing in EU server. I identify myself as a hardcore player, always strive to become better at this game and take most of the matches I participate in seriously. It doesn't mean that I don't know how to have fun or troll but only when victory is assured. It is a game afterall and having fun is the utmost priority. But it is also a team game so makes sure your goal in a match aligns with other players. You may have fun by trolling but not every one in the team thinks the same.

My moto is : Everyone makes mistakes so every game is winnable, it depends on if your mentality is steady enough to think through during the struggle.

This is not really a tier list but rather my review list requested by one of my friend because they are curious about my perspective of everyone. Well Twice now ( 25/09/2024 Updated )

By the time I added this line, I had already purged my friendlist as I felt I had had too much leniency allowing people to ruin my gaming experience and show their true colors. The more you take, the less you have; the less you have, the more you appreciate. Quality over quantity.
