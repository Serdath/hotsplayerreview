Heroes of the Storm Players

This tier list is purely subjective and depends on my experience playing with that player. I won't talk about people with whom I have never played nor the one I have too few games to conduct a proper review.

My IGN is : DarkPrelateZ and I am currently playing in EU server. I identify myself as a hardcore player, always strive to become better at this game and take most of the matches I participate in seriously. It doesn't mean that I don't know how to have fun or troll but only when victory is assured. It is a game afterall and having fun is the utmost priority. But it is also a team game so makes sure your goal in a match alligns with other players. You may have fun by trolling but not every one in the team thinks the same.

My highest rank achieved is GrandMaster 18: 8148RP. My moto is : Everyone makes mistakes so every game is winnable, it depends on if you truly want to win or not or if your mentality is steady enough to think through during the struggle.

This is not really a tier list but rather my review list requested by one of my friend because they are curious about my perspective of everyone.

I enjoy playing with

FrozenZerg

He is by far the most conscious Quick Match player I have ever met. Despite playing only Tychus and sometimes Butcher, He is willing to do whatever necessary to facilitate team's advantages by soaking or taking camps if his teammates happen to drown in ARAM too much. He also displays exceptional skills and adaptabilities with his favorite hero Tychus so you can always be assured he is fulfilling his role competently. From my experience, he is a hardcore player, focuses on winning rather than messing around and trolling, just like my style. I enjoy playing with him.

SamDovl

His Chromie is quite impressive, and he is an outgoing person, evident by constantly spamming emote at the start and at the end of the game. He also asks me (or you) nicely to join his lobby sometimes, which is appreciated instead of immediately inviting the player. He loves hunting Abathur and will gladly dive a fort to do so. Although sometimes by doing those risky moves, he forgets to soak, clear waves, and maybe even die. Still, he times his Time Traps perfectly to save his teammates multiple times. I enjoy playing in the same lobby with him.

I like playing with

Lipstick

Despite maining a toxic hero like Valeera, she is a friendly person to talk to and has also exceptional skills with her favorite heroes. However, as you can expect from a Valeera player, she will sit in one place and tries to assassinate stragglers rather than do camp and soak wave although she will still do so if she can't find an opportunity or their composition counters her but hey, that is what valeera's playstyle is and is good at so she is in character, no complaints. Her other heroes's skills range from decent to abysmal so expect her to int most of the time when she is not on her main. I like playing with her.

Neutral

Angela

I have heard and understood a lot of people complaining about her abusing the Match Making and playing in a specific composition to win games easily and boost her winrates. In my eyes, she uses and plays what the game has to offer and it is completely fine to me as long as she has fun and doesn't personally interfere with anyone else. although there were a few times I solo queued against her stack with 4 randoms and had kinda mix opinions about the game: The randoms were so incompetent and oblivious that they really deserved to lose, but some of the stack 5 players also played so poorly ( Twister/Ripper ) that should have been punished severely but got away with that anyway due to the former reasons as well as having good team composition and coordination. I have never seen her play another hero except Abathur so I can't say much about her overall skill levels but her abathur is only good because I have seen better. Playing in her lobby can be quite stressful because she won't tolerate a lost game but chances are you won't be playing in her lobby if she doesn't trust you. I digress with a neutral.
